About me

My name is Konstantinos Tsiamitros, and I am a student at Computer Engineering and Informatics Department of University of Patras.
I believe I've reached a point in my studies, where I can start giving back to the community that helped me learn programming.

Thus, I created this blog.
It will facilitate a series of lessons in the general principles of programming which can be applied to any language.
The aim is to build a sufficient background, so that you can go through the exersizes provided, without looking at the answers.

I like taking on a new challenge from time to time, and this endeavor will most definately teach me a lot as well!
Hopefully, you will have as much fun going through the material, as I had writting it! :)
